Pivoting

Early Stage Pivoting

At first, our team's idea was tracking.
Then, we narrowed it down to tracking kids (where it is a device used as an alarm if the tracking device gets taken off of the child).
Then, we changed it to tracking elders' daily activities and send a report to their care giver/children.
Finally, we changes the persona and market to focus on tracking VIPs at sport, music, and other events to provide them with the best service and give them the best VIP experience.

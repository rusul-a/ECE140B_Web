Our Product

Tracklet

For Whom

  • Elderly Individual
  • Others with medical conditions who need caregiver attention

What It Does

Collects data such as:
  • Steps
  • Time spent in a position/room
  • Time spent for an activity
  • Activity log
From the data, our system starts to recognize the pattern of lifestyle and activities the user has. Then if it recognizes any abnormality, it will send alerts to:
  • Family members
  • Other legal caregiver

How It Works

Using Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition, we can deliver a peace of mind regarding your loved ones. Our technology tracks user's positions and activities to make sure everything is normal with the user.

Key Benefits

  • Find out if the user daily activities are normal compared to other days
  • Have peace of mind about your loved parent/grandparent

Our Product

Banglet

Banglet is a smart bracelet product that offers venues access to VIP attendee locations to enable services such as concierge (food and drink delivery). When it comes to end-users, the bracelet could enable touch payments, seamless identity check for access to VIP-restricted areas and ordering drinks, as well as potentially an option to find points and persons of interest (bathrooms, medical team, other bracelet users).

For Whom

  • Event venues offering VIP option

What It Does

Collects data such as:
  • Guests' location
  • Check-ins at Vendors
  • Amount of money spent on which vendors
From the data, our system analyzes and models the data collected and present it to the event planner to help the planner enhance the VIP experience they provide to guests.

How It Works

Using UWB (Ultra-Wideband, used for short range communication) as our communication technology to connect the bracelets to our servers, each individual bracelet will carry the user/guest info and the bracelet will send location and purchase info to our server for analysis. At the same time, the bracelet can be used as identification and payment tool where the user can upload their info in advance to their banglet account and have their info available on the QR code on their Banglet.

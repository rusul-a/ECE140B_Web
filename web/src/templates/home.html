Product Idea

We have two product ideas in mind:

  • Smart Phone Alarm: This Device notifies you if somebody is in contact with your personal belongings. To use it, put the device on your personal item and get notified when the device senses vibration/change of orientation if the item is not in proximity of your phone and lets you activate the alarm to deter the potential tamperer. The extra benefit you could get from this device is if the personal item is lost, you can find it using the same technology used in AirTags/Tiles.

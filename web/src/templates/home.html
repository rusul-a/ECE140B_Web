Product Ideas

Our Current Product Idea:

  • Activity Tracking Bracelet (Tracklet): This bracelet is a wearable for elderly people who have some medical conditions and/or they live by themselves but need attention from family members or other caregiver. The device uses Machine Learning and Pattern Recognition to learn about the user's lifestyle including their activities and recognize a pattern then report any abnormalities to the learned pattern to the caregiver and alert them for attention.

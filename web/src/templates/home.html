Product Ideas

We have two product ideas in mind:

  • Smart Phone Alarm: This Device notifies you if somebody is in contact with your personal belongings. To use the device, put it on your personal item and get notified when the device senses vibration/change of orientation and location if the item is not in proximity of your phone and lets you activate the alarm to report to you the potential tamperer. The extra benefit you could get from this device is if the personal item is lost, you can find it using the same technology used in AirTags/Tiles.
